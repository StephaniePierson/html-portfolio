The Best Book Series According to Stephanie

The top 3 series worthy of many re-reads.

Harry Potter

I appreciate how the series becomes darker with each intalment; not only in theme, but in the loss of innocense of character and viewer.

Lord of the Rings

A timeless classic. This hero's journey highlights self-development and the importance of companionship.

Divergent (Series)

A female protagonist who destroys the balance of the existing world by unapologetically being herself.
